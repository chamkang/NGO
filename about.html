About Us

Welcome to LES AMIS SOLIDAIRES DE GATINEAU

Founded December 2, 2023

LES AMIS SOLIDAIRES DE GATINEAU is an apolitical, non-profit association officially founded at our constituent Annual General Meeting on December 2, 2023. We are a community-focused organization dedicated to promoting and encouraging development and social progress within our community.

Our association is built on the foundation of mutual aid and solidarity among members, specifically through organizing collective contributions and support for both happy and unhappy life events such as marriage, birth, illness, or death. We believe in the power of community support and collective action to create positive change.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote individual and collective development at all levels within our community, while encouraging and celebrating all forms of social progress through mutual aid and solidarity.

Our Vision

A strong, supportive community where every member feels valued and supported through life's journey, fostering an environment of collective growth and social progress.

Our Core Values

Benevolence

Acting with kindness, compassion, and goodwill towards all community members in every interaction.

Solidarity

Standing together in unity, providing mutual support and collective strength in times of need.

Mutual Aid

Providing reciprocal assistance and support, ensuring no member faces life's challenges alone.

What We Focus On

Mutual Aid & Solidarity
