Supporting New Immigrants

Building Bridges in Canadian Communities: Our Immigrant Support Program

Every year, thousands of newcomers arrive in Canada with dreams of building a better life. At LES AMIS SOLIDAIRES, we believe that successful integration benefits not just immigrants, but our entire Canadian community.

The Challenge: Navigating a New Country

When Ahmed arrived in Gatineau from Syria with his family of four, he faced the same challenges that many newcomers encounter: language barriers, unfamiliar job markets, and cultural differences. Despite having an engineering degree, he struggled to find employment and felt isolated in his new community.

"I felt lost in my own life. I had skills but couldn't communicate them effectively. LES AMIS SOLIDAIRES didn't just teach me English – they helped me rebuild my confidence."

Ahmed Hassan, Program Graduate

Our Comprehensive Support System

Our immigrant support program addresses the multifaceted challenges newcomers face through four key pillars:

Language & Communication
  • • ESL classes (beginner to advanced)
  • • Professional communication workshops
  • • Conversation practice groups
  • • Industry-specific language training
Employment Support
  • • Resume writing and interview preparation
  • • Credential recognition assistance
  • • Job placement partnerships
  • • Mentorship programs
Cultural Integration
  • • Canadian culture orientation
  • • Community connection events
  • • Family support services
  • • Youth programs for children
Practical Assistance
  • • Healthcare system navigation
  • • Banking and financial literacy
  • • Housing assistance
  • • Legal aid referrals
